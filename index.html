Cambrian Limestone Aquifer — Daly Basin

3D view of the four aquifer formations (Tindall, Jinduckin, Oolloo, Florina) and the Katherine Water Allocation Plan boundaries — the 2024–2026 plan area and the proposed 2026–2036 plan area (13 May 2026 package).

Rotate: left-drag   Pan: right-drag   Zoom: scroll

Use the panel on the right to toggle layers and basemap, and to adjust vertical exaggeration. With a basemap on, Tindall shows as a flat slab; with basemap off, it shows as a contour-interpolated 3D surface coloured by depth.
